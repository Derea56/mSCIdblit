# Module 12A: Innate Immune Conditioning and Tolerance Biology

## Overview

This tracker captures the first-pass corpus for innate immune conditioning, tolerance, and trained/tolerized host-state biology relevant to SCI reconstruction and neuroprotection. LPS/endotoxin tolerance remains an important mechanistic anchor, but the module is broader than LPS and broader than spinal cord injury: it includes TLR agonists, cytokine/adaptor routing, peripheral monocyte and endothelial priming, microbiome/systemic inflammatory conditioning, spinal ischemia, brain ischemia, TBI, and other comparator systems when they clarify regulatory nodes.

This module is intentionally framed as a mechanistic discovery module. LPS is tracked as one way to uncover endogenous protective programs and regulatory nodes, not as a proposed clinical therapeutic. Translation should route through safer host-conditioning strategies once mechanisms are defined.

**First-pass curation freeze**: 2026-07-20. Status: SATURATED / FROZEN FIRST PASS.
