# Module 11A: Spinal Cord Ischemic Conditioning in Cardiac and Aortic Surgery

## Overview

This tracker captures the first-pass saturated corpus for spinal cord ischemic conditioning and spinal cord protection in cardiac, thoracic aortic, thoracoabdominal aortic, endovascular aortic, and related high-risk vascular surgery contexts.

The module is adjacent to Module 7 host-conditioning evidence, Module 6 human translational pathology/imaging, Module 8 in vivo model systems, and Module 10 clinical trials, but remains separate because the target injury mechanism is perioperative spinal cord ischemia rather than traumatic SCI reconstruction.

**First-pass curation freeze**: 2026-07-20. Status: SATURATED / FROZEN FIRST PASS.
