# Module 4A: Cell Transplantation and Graft Integration After SCI — Corpus Building Tracker

## Overview

This tracker monitors experiment-level evidence on transplanted cells and graft integration after spinal cord injury, including neural stem/progenitor cells, Schwann cells, olfactory ensheathing cells, MSCs, iPSC-derived grafts, bone marrow cells, and combined scaffold-cell strategies.

**Target Corpus**: 30-60 primary research papers + 8-15 reviews, with emphasis on cell source, graft survival, differentiation, host-graft connectivity, immunology, safety, and recovery outcomes.

## Scope

| Include | Exclude or Defer |
|---|---|
| Cell transplant studies in SCI with histology, anatomy, electrophysiology, or functional outcomes | Pure in vitro cell-characterization papers without SCI repair relevance |
| Stem/progenitor, Schwann, OEC, MSC, BMMC, iPSC-derived, and fetal/embryonic grafts | Non-spinal CNS graft studies unless SCI is a direct comparator |
| Scaffold-cell combination papers | Scaffold-only papers stay primarily in Module 2A |
| Human clinical transplant/scaffold studies | Reviews only after primary search phases establish the map |
