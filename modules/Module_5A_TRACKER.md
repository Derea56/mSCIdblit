# Module 5A: Axon Regeneration, Remyelination, and White Matter Repair After SCI — Corpus Building Tracker

## Overview

This tracker monitors experiment-level evidence on axon regeneration, sprouting, remyelination, oligodendrocyte-lineage repair, spared white matter preservation, and white matter tract plasticity after spinal cord injury.

**Target Corpus**: 30-60 primary research papers + 8-15 reviews, with emphasis on tract identity, lesion context, regeneration versus sprouting, myelin/oligodendrocyte outcomes, and functional linkage.

## Scope

| Include | Exclude or Defer |
|---|---|
| CST, RST, sensory, propriospinal, and other tract regeneration/sprouting studies | Peripheral nerve regeneration unless directly bridged into SCI |
| Remyelination, oligodendrocyte progenitor responses, spared white matter repair | Pure motor-training papers without anatomical/repair readout |
| Inhibitory-matrix, intrinsic-growth, scaffold/graft-supported axon repair | Reviews only after primary search phases establish the map |
| Human white matter imaging/pathology when tied to repair or degeneration | Human imaging-only lesion morphology stays primarily in Module 6A |
