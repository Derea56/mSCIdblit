# Module 3A: Immune and Myeloid Modulation After SCI - Corpus Building Tracker

## Overview

This tracker monitors experiment-level evidence on immune and myeloid mechanisms after spinal cord injury, including microglia, macrophages, monocytes, neutrophils, cytokine programs, depletion/recruitment strategies, polarization language, and immune-modifying therapies.

**Target Corpus**: 30-60 primary research papers + 8-15 reviews, with emphasis on cell identity, timing, intervention, lesion effect, and functional consequence.

## Scope

| Include | Exclude or Defer |
|---|---|
| Microglia, macrophage, monocyte, neutrophil, and inflammatory-cell studies in SCI | Brain-only injury unless SCI is a direct comparator |
| Depletion, recruitment, receptor/signaling perturbation, cytokine modulation, and immune-modifying nanoparticles | Generic inflammation biomarker papers without experiment-level mechanism |
| Acute through chronic SCI immune trajectories | Pure clinical serum-marker papers unless linked to lesion/imaging outcomes |
| Immune-glial containment and scarring interactions | Reviews only after primary search phases establish the map |
